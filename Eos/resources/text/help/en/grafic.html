Grafic

Grafics own all properties and methods of the classes Figure, LineFigure , FilledFigure and Rectangle. A grafics object paints, after filling the area, an image into the rectangle. If the rectangle has different proportions as the image, you hvae to decide which tradeoff you want. Either the image is stretched , cut off or the rectangle isn't covered entirly.

Methods

loadImage(filename:String)

Loads the file as image. The image is searched relative to the location of the Eos file. Therefore you have to save your file once or you did load it.

stretchImage()

The image is stretched or shrinked to fit the size of the rectangle. The proportions of the image are changed.

cutImage()

The image is stretched (or shrinked) until it covers the whole rectangle area. Unused parts of the image are cut off.

fitImage()

The image is strechted (or shrinked) until the whole image is visible. Parts of the rectangle may stay empty as the proportions of the image are kept. That behaviour is similar to the black columns on a 16:9 tv showing a 4:3 show. Only the black columns here are transparent.

Example

image:Grafic
image.loadImage("reifen.png")
image.setWidth(40)
image.cutImage()
image.fitImage()
image.stretchImage()
image.setHeight(80)
image.cutImage()
image.fitImage()
image.stretchImage()
image.setHeight(40)
image.loadImage("blume.png")
image.cutImage()
image.fitImage()
image.stretchImage()
repeat 360 times
    image.rotate(1)
*repeat
