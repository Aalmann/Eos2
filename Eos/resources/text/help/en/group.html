Group

Groups own all properties and methods of the class Figure.

Groups have the purpose to assemble multiply object to one. Every call to group will influence every part of it. For example you could build a car using multiple circles and rectangles and add them to a group. That way you can move the car as a whole.

Remark: It is possible to change objects that are part of a group, but the results may be confusing. A figure outside a group moves relative to the containing window. A figure that is part of a group will move relativ to that group.

Methods

setBorderColor(color:Color)

Changes the border color of all figures within the group.

setBorderStyle(style:LineStyle)

Changes the border style of all figures within the group.

setBorderWidth(width:Number)

Changes the border width of all figures within the group.

setFillStyle(style:FillStyle)

Changes the fill style of all figures within the group.

setFillColor(color:Color)

Changes the fill color of all figures within the group.

consume(figure:Figure)

The figure is added to the group.

setCenter(x:Number,y:Number)

Sets the center of the group to the position (x, y). Every method using the center (rotate, strech, ...) will now use that point. Before calling that method the center of a group is the gravity center of all figures within the group.

copy(figure:Figure)

This call makes a copy of the figure and adds the copy to the group.

mirrorX()

Mirrors the group at the y axis.

mirrorY()

Mirrors the group at the x axis.

Example

c:Circle
g:Group
g.setCenter(0,0)
c.move(50,0)
repeat 11 times
   g.copy(c)
   g.rotate(30)
*repeat
g.cosume(c)
g.setBorderColor(blue)
repeat 1000 times
   g.rotate(1)
   g.setFillColor(red)
   g.setFillColor(yellow)
*repeat
