Squares

Squares own all properties and methods of the classes Figure, LineFigure and FilledFigure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Methods

setTopLeft(x:Number,y:Number)

Sets the upper left corner of the square.

Properties

side:Number

Length of the side of the square.

Example

s:Square
s.move(20,20)
s.side  = 15
s.stretch(2)
s.rotate(30)
