Figure

The figure is the base class for all geometric classes. That means every geometric class owns all methods and properties figure does. A circle and a rectangle are both figures therefore both can be moved, streched and so on.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Methods

move(dx:Number,dy:Number)

Moves the figure dx in x direction and dy in y direction.

stretch(factor:Number)

Stretches the figure at its center with the stretching factor.

moveTo(x:Number,y:Number)

Moves the figure to the position (x,y).

rotate(angle:Number)

Rotates the figure around its center counter clockwise for angle degrees.

rotateAround(x:Number,y:Number,angle:Number)

Rotates the figure around the point (x,y) counter clockwise for angle degrees.

Properties

visible:Boolean

Stores if the figure shall be drawn.

x:Number

The x coordinate of the figure (center).

y:Number

The y coordinate of the figure (center).

Example

r:Rectangle
c:Circle
c.move( 10 , 10 )
r.moveTo(- 20 , 0 )
repeat  10  times
   c.rotateAround( 20 , 0 , 10 )
   r.rotateAround( 20 , 0 , 10 )
*repeat
c.stretch( 2 )
r.stretchAt( 0 , 0 , 2 )
