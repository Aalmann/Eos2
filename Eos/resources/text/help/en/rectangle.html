Rectangle

Rectangles own all properties and methods of the classes Figure, LineFigure and FilledFigure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Methods

setLeftTop(left:Number, top:Number)

Sets the upper left corner of the rectangle.

setRightBottom(right:Number, bottom:Number)

Sets the lower right corner of the rectangle.

setCorners(left:Number, top:Number, right:Number, bottom:Number)

Changes the borders of the rectangle. The parameters left and right imply the new width. The parameters top and bottom imply the new height.

Properties

height:Number

The height of the rectangle.

width:Number

The width of the rectangle.

top:Number

The y coordinate of the upper border of the rectangle.

left:Number

The x coordinate of the left border of the rectangle.

bottom:Number

The y coordinate of the lower border of the rectangle.

right:Number

The x coordinate of the right border of the rectangle.

Example

box:Rectangle
box.left = 0
box.right = 40
box.top = 60
box.bottom = -20
