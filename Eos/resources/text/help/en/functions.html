Functions

EOS has a lot of predefined mathematic functions, that can be used at any place where a number is needed.

round(value:number):number

Rounds a number.

trunc(value:number):number

Cuts of all numbers after the decimal point.

random(from:number, to:number):number

RGenerates a random number between from and to including both bounds.

abs(value:number):number

Returns the positive value.

sqrt(value:number):number

Calculates the square root.

phi(x:number, y:number):number

Returns the angle relativ to the x axis.

r(x:number, y:number):number

Returns the distance to the origin.

sin(angle:number):number

Calculates the sinus of the angle in degree.

cos(angle:number):number

Calcualtes the cosinus of the angle in degree.

tan(angle:number):number

Calculates the tangens of the angle in degree.

arcsin(x:number):number

Calculates the inverse sinus in degree.

arccos(x:number):number

Calculates the inverse cosinus in degree.

arctan(x:number):number

Calculates the inverse tangens in degree.

t:Textfield
t.appendline(phi(2,3))
