Fillstyle

The fillstyle determines the style and technic that is used to fill areas. There are several values.

  • filled

    • The area is filled.

  • ruled

    • The area is ruled.

  • checked

    • The area is checked.

  • transparent

    • The area is empty.

Usage

The fillstyle is needed for the following properties and methods.
  • FilledFigure.setFillStyle(style:FillStyle)
  • FilledFigure.getFillStyle(style:FillStyle)
  • FilledFigure.fillstyle:FillStyle

Example

c:Circle
c.setFillStyle(checked)

BNF

Fillstyle := "filled" | "ruled" | "checked" | "transparent"
