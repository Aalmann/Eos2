Triangle

Triangles own all properties and methods of the classes Figure, LineFigure and FilledFigure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Methods

setCorners(x1:Number,y1:Number,x2:Number,y2:Number,x3:Number,y3:Number)

Sets all three corners of the triangle.

mirrorX()

Mirrors the triangle at the y axis.

mirrorY()

Mirrors the triangle at the x axis.

Examples

t:Triangle
t.setCorners(- 30 , 0 , 0 , 20 , 10 , 0 )
t.mirrorX()
t.mirrorY()
t.mirrorX()
t.mirrorY()
