Filled Figure

The filled figure is is the base class for all geometric figures that cover an area. Filled Figures own all properties and methods of the classes Figure and LineFigure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Properties

fillstyle:FillStyle

The style and technic that is used to fill the area.

fillcolor:Color

The color that is used to fill the area.

Example

ball:Circle
ball.fillcolor = blue
ball.fillstyle = filled
