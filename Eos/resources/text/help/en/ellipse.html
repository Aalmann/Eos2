Ellipse

Ellipses own all properties and methods of the classes Figure, LineFigure and FilledFigure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Methods

setCenter(x:Number,y:Number)

Sets the center to (x,y).

Properties

centerX:Number

The x coordinate of the center.

centerY:Number

The y coordinate of the center.

radiusX:Number

The horizontal radius of the ellipse.

radiusY:Number

The vertical radius of the ellipse.

Examples

eye:Ellipse
eye.radiusX = 10
eye.radiusY = 20
eye.setCenter(-10, 20)
