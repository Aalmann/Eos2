Borderstyle/Linestyle

The border style determines how the border line of a figure is drawn. There are the following values:

  • dashed
  • solid
  • dotted
  • dasheddotted
  • invisible

Usage

The BorderStyle/LineStyle is needed for the following methods and properties.
  • Group.setBorderStyle(style:LineStyle)
  • LineFigure.setBorderStyle(style:BorderStyle)
  • LineFigure.getBorderStyle():BorderStyle
  • LineFigure.borderStyle:BorderStyle
  • Line.getLineStyle():LineStyle
  • Line.setLineStyle(style:LineStyle)
  • Line.linestyle:LineStyle

Example

c:Circle
c.setBorderStyle(dotted)

BNF

BorderStyle := "dashed" | "solid" | "dotted" | "dasheddotted" | "invisible"
