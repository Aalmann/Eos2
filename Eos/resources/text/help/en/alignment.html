Alignment

The alignment defines how a text is placed within a rectangle. There are the following values:

  • centered

    • The text is central.

  • leftaligned

    • The text is placed at the left border.

  • rightaligned

    • The text is placed at the right border.

  • topaligned

    • The text is placed at the upper border.

  • bottomaligned

    • The text is placed at bottom border.

Usage

The alignment is needed for the following methods and properties.
  • Textfield.getAlignmentVertical():Alignment
  • Textfield.getAlignmentHorizontal():Ausrichtung
  • Textfield.setAlignmentVertical(alignment:Alignment)
  • Textfield.setAlignmentHorizontal(alignment:Alignment)
  • Textfield.alignmentVertical:Alignment
  • Textfield.alignmentHorizontal:Alignment

Example

text:Textfield
text.setAlignmentHorizontal( centered )
text.setAlignmentVertical( leftaligned )

BNF

Alignment := "centered" | "leftaligned" | "rightaligned" | "topaligned" | "bottomaligned"
