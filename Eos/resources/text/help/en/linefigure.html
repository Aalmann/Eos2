LineFigure

A line figure is the base calss for all figures with border. Line figures own all properties and methods of the classes Figure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Properties

borderColor:Color

The color of the border.

borderStyle:BorderStyle

The style of the border.

borderWidth:Number

The thickness of the border.
