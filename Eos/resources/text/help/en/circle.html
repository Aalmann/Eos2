Circle

Circles own all properties and methods of the classes Figure, LineFigure and FilledFigure.

Setter- and Getter-Methods

For every property generally there are two methods for setting and getting the property value.
eg x:Number ↔ getX():Number ↔ setX(x:Number)
They are not extra documented.

Methods

setCenter(x:Number,y:Number)

Sets the center at the position (x,y).

Properties

centerX:Number

The x coordinate of the center point.

centerY:Number

The y coordinate of the center point.

radius:Number

The radius of the circle.

Beispiel

c:Circle
c.centerX = 5
c.centerY = 4
c.setRadius(10)
c.setCenter(20,20)
